About Wave Cafe

Welcome to Sisi Kopi, where every cup tells a story of passion and dedication. Located in the heart of Padang, our café is more than just a place to enjoy a delicious brew—it's a community hub where coffee lovers gather to share experiences and create memories. At Sisi Kopi, we pride ourselves on sourcing the finest coffee beans, expertly roasted to perfection to deliver a rich and aromatic experience in every sip. Our menu offers a wide range of options, from classic favorites like Hot Latte and Iced Cappuccino to unique creations like our indulgent Cookies and Cream..

Misi kami adalah menyediakan suasana yang nyaman dan mengundang di mana para tamu dapat bersantai, bekerja, atau bertemu dengan teman-teman. Baik Anda datang untuk sekadar menyegarkan diri atau bersantai di sore hari, barista kami yang ramah berdedikasi untuk membuat Anda merasa nyaman.
